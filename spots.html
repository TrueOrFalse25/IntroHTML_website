Spots in NYC

Best Spots

Central Park is a beautiful park in Manhattan that is great for running/biking, and the nature all around it is beautiful.

The Statue of Liberty is a beautiful national landmark constructed by the French and given to the US that can be toured/visited.

Worst Spots

ANY porta-potty is one of the worst places in NYC. They are piles of many peoples' waste trapped in small stalls all around the city.

Crowded trains are extremely uncomfortable. They are very cramped and it can be hard to breathe your own air sometimes.

Other cool places in NYC are

  • the Empire State Building
  • Prospect Park
  • Coney Island/Luna Park
