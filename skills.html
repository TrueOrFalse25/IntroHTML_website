Jumproping

Hold each handle in each hand with the rope dangling behind you.

Keep your arms out and swing them in front of you. As the rope starts to get closer to your feet, jump off the ground.

As the rope goes behind you and you re-land on the ground, keep swinging your arms and jumping! And add some tricks too!
